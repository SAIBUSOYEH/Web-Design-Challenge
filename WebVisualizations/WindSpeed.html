Latitude vs. WindSpeed



The relative movement at which air travels, irrespective of the direction is called the windspeed. Wind is created when air moves from areas of high pressure toward areas where the air pressure is low. Wind speed varies around the world. The latitude amongst other factors determined the windspeed.

Windspeed from the graph decreases when closer to the equator.
